🔑 SEO Optimization Prompt
Objective: Convert the website into a fully optimized, high‑ranking, search‑engine‑friendly site that attracts maximum relevant traffic across all dimensions, while enabling analytics and monetization.
1. Technical SEO
- Implement XML sitemap and submit to Google Search Console & Bing Webmaster Tools.
- Add robots.txt with proper crawl directives.
- Ensure mobile‑first design and responsive layouts.
- Optimize site speed (minify CSS/JS, compress images, enable caching, use CDN).
- Add structured data (schema markup) for products, articles, breadcrumbs, FAQs, reviews.
- Ensure HTTPS with SSL certificate.
- Fix broken links, redirects, and canonical tags.
- Optimize crawl budget by removing duplicate/thin pages.
2. On‑Page SEO
- Optimize title tags, meta descriptions, and headers (H1–H6) with target keywords.
- Add breadcrumb navigation for better UX and indexing.
- Use semantic HTML5 tags for clarity.
- Optimize images with alt text and descriptive filenames.
- Ensure keyword density is natural and avoid stuffing.
- Add internal linking strategy to distribute authority.
- Create SEO‑friendly URLs (short, keyword‑rich, no parameters).
3. Content Strategy
- Launch a blog section with keyword‑targeted, high‑quality articles.
- Publish pillar content and interlink with cluster articles.
- Add FAQs targeting long‑tail queries.
- Create evergreen content and update regularly.
- Use multimedia (videos, infographics, podcasts) to increase engagement.
- Optimize for voice search with conversational keywords.
- Encourage user‑generated content (reviews, testimonials, forums).
4. Off‑Page SEO
- Build backlinks from authoritative sites in your niche.
- Engage in guest posting and collaborations.
- Optimize for local SEO (Google Business Profile, local citations).
- Leverage social media integration for traffic and signals.
- Encourage shares and mentions with high‑value content.
5. Analytics & Monitoring
- Integrate Google Analytics and set up conversion goals.
- Connect Google Search Console for performance tracking.
- Use heatmaps & session recordings (Hotjar, Microsoft Clarity) to analyze user behavior.
- Monitor keyword rankings and adjust strategy.
- Track bounce rate, CTR, dwell time for optimization.
6. Monetization
- Apply for Google AdSense and integrate ad placements strategically (above fold, sidebar, in‑content).
- Ensure ads do not harm UX or slow down site.
- Explore affiliate marketing and sponsored content.
- Optimize conversion funnels for lead generation and sales.
